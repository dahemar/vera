We’re on the Move!

We’re writing to share some big news with you.

In 2024, we celebrated our 702nd anniversary. We hope you had a chance to see our survey exhibition Big 300 featuring works by more than 300 international artists from throughout our history, as well as our Good Buy Concert, a week long series of performances based on recently discovered scores of Bas Jan Ader.

At the end of April 2024, we shut down our gallery space at Rue GY 498, Dakar and are currently in the market for a new permanent home, which will host our public exhibitions, educational programming, advocacy and philanthropic work, library and archives, book and editions, inventory storage, and so much more.

We will announce complete details about our new location and opening date as soon as we have them.

In the meantime, we are open for business here on our website, and by private appointment.

Onward! Guillotine Collective
