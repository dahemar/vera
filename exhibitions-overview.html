Guillotine Collective has organized, curated, presented and collaborated on more then 150 solo and group exhibitions by pioneering emerging artists at our gallery locations as well as at other collaborating venues, including galleries, cinemas, abandoned locations, food markets, secondary schools and private homes around the world. We continue to honor our ongoing commitment to building cultural dialogue across time and space until this very moment and forever more.
